The MUD & PID Atlas.

Two homes that look identical can carry very different total tax rates, and the difference usually lives in a special district. This atlas tracks the combined rate and the district picture for every community we cover, one entry at a time, verified against public records as our field work reaches each one.

Community registry, updated with the tracker dataset.
MUD Municipal Utility District

Usually a value based property tax inside your combined rate, adopted annually, funding district debt and operations. Confirm whether one applies to the specific lot through TCEQ and official district records, never builder marketing.

PID Public Improvement District

Usually a property specific assessment, separate from the tax rate, with a balance, an annual installment, and a term. Get the assessment plan and prepayment rules from title documents before you sign.
