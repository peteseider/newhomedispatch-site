The Incentive Reality Calculator

Builders advertise incentives in big numbers — a rate buydown, a closing-cost credit, "free" upgrades. This shows what that package is actually worth on your home over the years you'll keep the loan, and whether taking the in-house-lender deal really beats simply shopping the loan yourself.
