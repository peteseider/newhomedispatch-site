Free Tool · Dot — named for the period in “Look Closer.”

Meet Dot. Ask the data, not a salesperson.

Dot is the Dispatch Assistant. She answers from New Home Dispatch’s own datasets — tracked incentives, verified tax rates, the entry-level price floor, and every builder and community we cover. It doesn’t guess, it doesn’t sell, and when it doesn’t know, it says so and points you to a human. Nothing you type leaves this page.

This assistant runs entirely in your browser on this site’s published datasets — the same numbers on the tracker and profile pages, with the same source labels. Payment figures are illustrative estimates, never quotes. It is educational, not financial or legal advice, and it will tell you when a question needs a professional instead of a dataset.

Some decisions need a human on your side.

The data gets you to a smart shortlist. Negotiating a specific contract, reading a specific lot, timing a specific builder — that’s judgment work. A free, no-obligation strategy call with the Seider Realty Team (the buyer-representation side of this platform, disclosed on every page) puts an expert on your side of the table.
