Look Closer.

25 things to check before you fall in love with a new-home community.

Print / save PDF ↓ Use it on your phone ↓
0 / 25 checked Nothing is saved — it resets when you leave.

A model home and a good afternoon can sell almost anyone. This is a calm list to slow you down and help you notice what matters — while you are still standing there. Tap each box as you go, photograph anything you'll want to remember, and write down the questions that get vague answers.

1. Start with the drive

How you'll actually live here begins at the highway exit.

2. Read the edges

The perimeter tells you more than the entrance.

3. Walk an ordinary street

The model is the best face — go find the everyday one.

4. Ask what's still coming

A community is a moving target — record the plan.

5. Look closely inside the model

The model is built to sell — know what's actually included.

6. Understand the real monthly cost

The sticker price is only part of the picture.

7. Before you leave

The follow-up questions are where clarity comes from.

The whole idea
A website can be read in a minute. The sound of the highway from a back lot, the gap between the model and the street behind it, the answer that changed when you asked twice — none of that shows up online. What you can see in person is often exactly what a website won't show you. That's why it's worth going to look closer, in person, before you decide.

If you'd like a second set of eyes before you commit, New Home Dispatch publishes independent, on-the-ground Community Field Notes across Austin and Central Texas. When you're ready, you can talk it through with us — no pressure, no rush, just an honest read.

This checklist is educational information to help you observe a community firsthand — it is not tax, legal, or financial advice, and it does not rate or endorse any builder or community. Confirm tax rates, MUD/PID status, HOA dues, and incentive terms with official sources and in writing before relying on them. New Home Dispatch is independent and not affiliated with any builder.
